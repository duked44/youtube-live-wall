One-time setup: caption the wall's audio, not your room
  1. Press Win+R, run mmsys.cpl, open the Recording tab.
  2. Right-click the empty list area → Show Disabled Devices.
  3. Right-click Stereo Mix → Enable, then Set as Default Device.
  4. Click Start captions above and allow microphone access.

Now the transcript captures whatever feed has the audio (the white ring). No Stereo Mix on your PC? The free VB-CABLE driver (vb-audio.com) does the same job, or plain speakers + microphone works in a quiet room. Heads up: Chrome's free recognizer sends the audio to Google's speech service to transcribe it.
